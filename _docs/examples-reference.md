# Examples Reference — Other Businesses' First 3 Hires

> Short illustrative list of "here's what other businesses hired" — NOT a fixed kit, NOT a menu the student has to pick from. The Chief of Staff dynamically generates the right hires for each student based on their diagnostic.
>
> This page exists to spark ideas + point students at the Academy's growing **Role Library** for pre-built starters when they want a head-start.

## How to use this page

1. Read through the examples below to see what's possible
2. Run the Chief of Staff diagnostic (Day 1 Lesson 4) — Chief of Staff will recommend YOUR top 2 hires based on your specific situation
3. Optionally browse the **Role Library** in the Academy for pre-built role specs you can customize

## Examples

### Wellness brand (e.g., Plant Based Tone — our course demo)

**Hires:** Content Designer + Web Designer

- **Content Designer** produces meal plan lists, recipes, workshops, wellness guides, lead magnets
- **Web Designer** refreshes homepage, builds email signup popup, creates seasonal landing pages (Mother's Day, holiday promos), fixes social link gaps

### Healthcare practice (e.g., functional medicine, chiropractic)

**Common hires:** Content Designer + Admin Assistant

- **Content Designer** writes patient education content, condition-specific guides, intake forms
- **Admin Assistant** handles scheduling, email triage, file organization

### Home services (e.g., roofing, painting, detailing)

**Common hires:** Research & Estimate Specialist + Admin Assistant

- **Research & Estimate Specialist** compares vendor pricing, verifies estimates, sources materials
- **Admin Assistant** handles lead intake, scheduling, follow-up reminders

### Financial advisor

**Common hires:** Content Designer + Admin Assistant

- **Content Designer** produces market commentary, client educational content, newsletter
- **Admin Assistant** manages calendar, document filing, client onboarding workflow

### E-commerce brand

**Common hires:** Web Designer + Social Media Manager *(future Library drop — see Coming Soon below)*

- **Web Designer** updates product pages, builds promo landing pages, refreshes homepage seasonally
- **Social Media Manager** schedules platform-native content, manages community engagement

### Coach / consultant

**Common hires:** Content Designer + Workshop Builder *(future Library drop)*

- **Content Designer** creates lead magnets, blog posts, email sequences
- **Workshop Builder** designs workshops, slide decks, training programs
