# Role-Specific Clarifying Questions

Ask 3-5 of these per role. Adapt to what the user already said in the diagnostic.

## Content Designer

1. What kinds of content? (Blog posts, recipes, lead magnets, social captions, all of the above)
2. Publishing cadence? (Weekly blog, daily social, occasional lead magnets)
3. Topics or themes that are off-limits?
4. What's the FIRST piece of content you want them to produce?
5. Length preferences? (Short and punchy / detailed and comprehensive)

## Web Designer

1. What kind of web work? (New pages, redesigns, landing pages, email popups, all of the above)
2. What platform is your site on? (WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Shopify, Webflow, custom)
3. What's your brand color palette? (Or do you want them to pull from your existing site?)
4. What's the FIRST web project you want them to tackle?
5. Mobile-first or desktop-first?

## Social Media Manager

1. Which platforms? (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Facebook, X, YouTube, all of the above)
2. Posting cadence per platform? (Daily, every-other-day, weekly)
3. What kind of content? (Educational, behind-the-scenes, promotional, all)
4. What's your account size on each platform? (helps calibrate ambition)
5. What's the FIRST week of content you want them to produce?

## Admin Assistant

1. What admin work? (Calendar, email triage, document filing, scheduling, expense tracking)
2. What tools do you use? (Google Workspace, Office 365, both)
3. Anything you want them to NEVER touch? (Specific email senders, certain calendar slots)
4. What's the FIRST admin task you want them to tackle?

## Research & Estimate Specialist

1. What kind of research? (Vendor pricing, market data, estimate verification, competitor research)
2. What industry/niche?
3. What's your decision criteria when comparing options?
4. What's the FIRST research project you want them to tackle?

## Workshop Builder

1. Workshop format? (Live webinar, recorded course, in-person event, mixed)
2. Topic of the FIRST workshop you want them to design?
3. Length preferences? (30-min taster, 90-min deep dive, 4-hour intensive, multi-session)
4. Who's the audience?

## Video Producer

1. Video format? (Long-form YouTube, short-form TikTok/Reels, both)
2. Recording setup? (You record yourself, you have a videographer, AI-generated)
3. What's your editing style? (Fast cuts / slow + thoughtful / mix)
4. What's the FIRST video project?

## Lead Researcher

1. Who are you researching? (Companies, individuals, both — be specific about ICP)
2. What sources can they use? (LinkedIn, company websites, news, all of the above)
3. What's the qualifying criteria? (Budget, role, company size, geography)
4. How many leads per week is the target?

## Customer Service Responder

1. Where do customer messages come in? (Email, social DMs, contact form, multiple)
2. What kinds of messages? (Support tickets, sales inquiries, both)
3. What's the response time goal?
4. When should they escalate to you vs. handle solo?

## Bookkeeping Helper

1. What accounting software? (QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, spreadsheet)
2. What tasks? (Categorize transactions, invoice clients, monthly reports, all)
3. What's your fiscal year setup?

## Reputation Manager

1. Where do reviews/testimonials live? (Google, Yelp, social, your own site, all)
2. Goal: more reviews? Better testimonials? Negative review responses? All?
3. Tone for responses? (Warm and personal / professional and brief)

## Email Specialist

1. What sequences? (Welcome, onboarding, abandoned cart, lifecycle, broadcasts)
2. What ESP? (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, GHL, custom)
3. What's the FIRST sequence you want them to build?
4. List size + average open rate?

## App Designer

1. What kinds of tools? (Trackers, quizzes, calculators, intake forms, mini-apps)
2. For you only, or for your clients to use too?
3. Tech preference? (No-code platform, simple HTML page, embeddable widget)
4. What's the FIRST tool you want them to build?

## SEO Specialist

1. What's your site? (URL)
2. Goal? (Keyword rankings, organic traffic, conversions from organic, local SEO, all)
3. Where are you weakest? (Content, technical SEO, backlinks)

## When the role doesn't fit a known pattern

Ask:

1. "What does this Employee do, in one sentence?"
2. "What does success look like for them in their first month?"
3. "What's the FIRST thing you want them to do as a sanity check?"
4. "Anything they should NEVER do?"
5. "Who should they collaborate with — Chief of Staff only, or other Employees too?"
